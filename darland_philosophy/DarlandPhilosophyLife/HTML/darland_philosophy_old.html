    I will try to show how I handle the various issues concerning meaning, propositions, etc.
    I am going to try to show something positive - how I can handle the issues involved.
    I believe there are solutions. I think philosophers tend to be too negative.
    Also, you cannot expect to prove deductively that philosophical theories are true.
    You may be able to find contradictions within them - which shows some assumption is incorrect.
    But what the incorrect assumption is, as Whitehead pointed out, may not be clear.
    There are problem in logic and math for which it has been shown that there is no solution.
    But this is no reason to give up on other problems.
    I believe there are solutions to many perplexing problems.
